Why are cookies important?

This website uses its own and third-party cookies for its correct functioning.

What types of cookies does this website use?

technical cookies
They allow the use and navigation of the website. We do not use any such cookies.
customization cookies
They allow modifying the behavior or some characteristics of the website. We do not use any cookie of this type, but to show/hide the notice about the Cookies Policy, we store a configuration option in your browser.
Analysis cookies
They allow anonymous analysis of the users who visit us and their behavior. We do not use any cookies of this type, but this website uses a service called Google Analytics that does use these types of cookies. read the Google Analytics privacy politics.
Advertising cookies
They allow to show the most effective possible advertising for each user. We do not use any cookies of this type. read the Google Adsense privacy politics and you can also see adjust your advertising preferences.
